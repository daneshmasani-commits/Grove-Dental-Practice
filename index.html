About the practice

A familiar face on Plashet Grove

Grove Dental Practice has quietly looked after East Ham families for decades — the kind of place where the dentist knows your name, your history, and remembers to ask after your family. Led by Dr Syed Hussain, who has cared for local patients for over 25 years, we offer calm, unhurried treatment for every age, on the NHS and privately.
